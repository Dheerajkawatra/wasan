  • About Wasan Timber Merchant (WTM)

    Founded in year 1978, Wasan Timber Merchants undertakes turnkey office interior projects including partitions, all kind of steel and wooden furniture, cubicles and cabins, workstations, storage units, filing systems, complete range of chairs, industrial storage, false ceiling, flooring, wall paints (exteriors and interiors both), wall treatments, floor treatments, doors and windows, window dressings, landscape, acoustics and other special effects.We are leading stockist of all types of plywood namely Centuryply, Greenply, Vostro ,Armstrong, Merino, Century laminates and all forms of adhesives and wood like Nagpur teak, Ghana Teak, Australian Pine, New zealand Pine .We undertake a full range of general contracting and design services for a sophisticated and discerning clientele. WTM is one of the best turnkey project executor and architectural firm based in Gurgaon. Integrating the entire spectrum of interior design services as well as architectural services in Gurgaon, Delhi NCR including Noida, Ghaziabad, Faridabad and several other parts of India helped us emerge as one of the most popular name in the list of interior designers in India. We at WTM in Gurgaon are well equipped with the capabilities to conceptualize, execute and manage all our interior design projects on turnkey basis. For us, Interior Design begins with conceptualization and ends only after its successful execution. With a focus on high-end projects, we build long-term relationships with our clients through workmanship, expertise, and a commitment to quality and service. For these reasons, we approach each client project with the same level of commitment, whether we are providing services for a single storefront or for a large commercial property. Our organization is amongst the renowned service provider for supreme quality range of corporate services . Being a client centric organization, we are involved in offering a wide range of services. Our experienced professionals work on both traditional and contemporary designing themes as per the trendy and modern lifestyle. Furthermore, we also take care for space planning and offer these services at pocket friendly rates. We ensure that our services are of best quality, highly efficient, great ambience and executed on given time frame. we offer range of services and serve esteemed organizations .With over 3 decades of experience, our team of designers will take you from the beginning to end of your project. Each project is unique and we spend time to execute your project. To complement the efforts of our design team and execution team, we have got the services of experienced and competent consultants and workmen force to execute all kind of turnkey projects.
