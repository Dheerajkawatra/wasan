  • About Wasan Timber Merchant (WTM)

    Founded in year 1978, We M/S Wasan Timber Merchants are leading stockist of all types of plywood namely Centuryply, Greenply, Vostro , Merino and wood like Nagpur teak, Ghana Teak, malyasian Sal, New zealand Pine etc. and allied wooden products. We also undertake turnkey Residential and office interior projects.

    Our another specialised division is engaged in providing highend packaging solutions from over past three decades to various leading companies engaged in automobile, telecom, electrical sector spread over Gurgaon, Bhiwadi, Bawal, Neemrana and many other industrial belts .
